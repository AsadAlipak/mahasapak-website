About Mahasa Pakistan

Professional trading house established 1994, group formed 2002 with international representation.

Our Story

Mahasa Pakistan was established in 1994 as a professional trading house with a vision to provide world-class telecommunications and engineering solutions. Our journey began with a commitment to excellence and a focus on building long-term partnerships with international suppliers and local clients.

In 2002, we expanded our operations and formed the Mahasa Pakistan Group, bringing together six specialized companies to serve diverse sectors including telecommunications, medical equipment, and international trade. This strategic growth enabled us to offer comprehensive solutions across multiple industries.

Today, with offices in Pakistan, USA, and China, and partnerships with 25 overseas principals, we continue to be a trusted name in telecommunications infrastructure, construction projects, energy solutions, and industrial equipment. Our success is measured by our $4.5M sales in 2010-2011 and our ambitious target of $6M for 2011-2012.
